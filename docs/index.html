ABOUT US

Steph and Zach met in Washington DC in January 2013.

Since then, they have lived in 5 cities over 4 years,
and have shared approximately 3,600 cups of coffee.

They currently live in Cambridge, Massachusetts.

Steph and Zach Meet

Arlington, Virginia

Steph and Zach meet for the first time in a coffee shop in Arlington, Virginia. Steph had said that she "lived in DC", so Zach was surprised to learn that, in reality, she lived across the river in Arlington.

January 19, 2013

First "I love you"s

Washington DC

Zach and Steph exchange "I love you"s for the first time. Steph began the conversation by saying "I love you, but it doesn't have to be a thing."

July 19, 2013

"Breakup Vacation"

Toronto, Canada

At this point, Steph and Zach are still planning on breaking up for graduate school, because they can't imagine being a long-distance couple indefinitely. Steph has a trip planned to Toronto to watch Kodi play frisbee, and invites Zach to join. Spoiler alert: the breakup vacation failed to accomplish its job.

July 23, 2013

Steph and Zach decide to try long-distance

Arlington, VA

Zach shows up to Steph's front door with flowers. They decide to try out a long-distance relationship. Zach promises to bring Steph coffee whenever he visits.

July 31, 2013

Steph Moves to Pittsburgh

Pittsburgh, PA

Steph moves to Pittsburgh to start "18th grade" in statistics.

September 5th(ish), 2013

Zach moves to Cambridge

Cambridge, MA

Zach moves to Cambridge to start "17th grade" in transportation engineering.

September 12(ish), 2013

First Christmas Together

Cambridge, MA

Steph and Zach exchange Christmas gifts for the first time. Zach gets Steph a knitted beer coozy.

December 20, 2013

Steph Moves to New York

New York City

Zach and Steph both travel for internships. Steph moves to New York, and Zach moves back to DC for the summer. Together, they pack up Zach's room in Cambridge and share part of the long drive from Cambridge to DC, by way of NYC. Zach drops Steph off on a corner in New York with two duffel bags, because he is unable to find a parking space, and is stressed about traffic.

May 31, 2014

Steph Moves to Cambridge

Cambridge, MA

After agonizing excessively about whether or not it was a "feminist decision", Steph accepts a job and moves to Cambridge. It is the first time Zach and Steph have lived in the same city, or within 40 minutes of each other via public transportation.

September 1, 2014

Record Snowfall in Boston

Boston, MA

During Steph's first winter in Massachusetts, the Boston area gets a record 108.6 in of snow.

January 20, 2015

Boston, MA

Steph and Zach travel back to California for the first time together. Steph's dad and Zach spit-roast a lamb to celebrate. After the trip, Steph and Zach travel back to Cambridge together for Zach's graduation from "18th grade".

May 23, 2015

Cupertino, CA

Zach smokes his first brisket.

July 22, 2017

Zach Proposes

Fort Foster State Park, Maine

Steph and Zach get engaged on a day-trip to Maine. They cannot stop smiling for the next two weeks.

August 26, 2017

WE'RE GETTING MARRIED!
